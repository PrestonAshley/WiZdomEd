Privacy Policy

We are aware that personal information is critical to the person it belongs to. Therefore, it is mandatory for us to put in place a privacy policy that describes in detail how we collect, handle and process the personal information collected by us. This privacy policy aims at increasing transperancy between us by describing in detail the type of information that is recorded by us and how we use it, thereby inspiring user confidence in us. As is true for most websites(and in the case of mobile applications), we also gather information about the user and store it in log files in order to record interactions between the user and our system.

Log files
We use our log files to hold a record of activity on our web server to identify the user of the site, including Internet Protocol (IP) addresses , operating system information, browser type, internet service provider, location, date and time of site visit and general visitor activity on the site. This information is used to measure user trends and track user movement in and around the site. Internet Protocol address are not linked to any data that is personally identifiable.

Contact and geolocation information – We may record information that the user may provide such as name, email, socia media handles, phone number, address

Purchase and preference information – we may collect information regarding the users order history, browsing history, trends and preferences.

Internet and network activity information – We may collect information that the user posts on our site, on any public platform, or any third party social media platform related to our company. We may also collect information that might include device identifiers, browsing and search history on our sites and what site the user visits before and after our site visit and includes user interactions with our digital ads.

Demographic information – We may collect data that provides us certain statitical information that reveals information regarding age, gender or any other information revealed during the use of our site. This information so collected may be used as part of our reviews, opinion polls and studies.

How is this information collected by us

Voluntarily – We record information users voluntarily and actively provide when they register to use the services of our site. We record user information when you contact us through phone or email or when you post a comment on our website.

Automatically – We also record information that we get automatically, such as server and http logs.
